The loop

Guess. Dodge. Collect.

Three seconds of decision, one tap, instant payoff. It's simple to learn and brutal to put down.

01

Guess the safe tile

Every round serves two numbers that look identical. One advances you. One springs the trap. No tells, no patterns to lean on — just your read.

02

Dodge the trap

Tap right and you bank points and refund a heart. Tap wrong and a heart's gone. The board re-mixes after every miss, so memorising won't save you.

03

Collect the relic

Fill the whole board to clear a level. In Adventure, every clear drops a unique relic disc into your vault — yours to keep forever.

Two ways to play

Pick your run.
