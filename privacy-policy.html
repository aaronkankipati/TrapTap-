Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Kankipati Aaron ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the mobile game Trap Tap (the "App"). By downloading or using the App, you agree to the practices described below. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

In short: Trap Tap has no accounts and no login. We do not ask you for your name, email, or any personal details. Your game progress is stored only on your own device. The only data leaving your device is collected by Google AdMob to display ads, as explained below.

1. Information stored on your device

Trap Tap saves your gameplay data locally on your device using your device's standard storage. This includes your high score, your current level progress, and your collection of in-game "tazo" rewards. This information stays on your device, is not transmitted to us, and is removed if you uninstall the App or clear its data.

2. Information collected by advertising (Google AdMob)

Trap Tap is free to play and displays advertisements through Google AdMob, a service provided by Google. To serve and measure ads, the Google Mobile Ads SDK may automatically collect and share certain information, including:

  • Device or other identifiers (such as the advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • App activity and interactions
  • Diagnostic and performance information

This data is used for advertising, analytics, and fraud prevention, and is transmitted using encryption (TLS). We do not control how Google processes this data. For details, please review Google's privacy practices and how Google uses information from partner apps:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services

3. How information is used

Information is used only to operate the App and to display advertising. Specifically, on-device data is used to save your progress and rewards, and AdMob data is used to deliver, personalize, and measure ads and to prevent fraud. We do not sell your personal information.

4. Sharing of information

We do not share personal information with third parties ourselves. The only data sharing that occurs is between Google's advertising service and Google's advertising partners, as described in Google's privacy documentation linked above.

5. Your choices

You can limit ad personalization at any time through your device settings (for example, by resetting or limiting your advertising ID under Android's Settings > Privacy > Ads). Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored game data from your device.

6. Data retention and deletion

We do not maintain any server-side account or database of users, so we hold no personal data to delete. On-device game data is retained until you clear it or uninstall the App. If you have questions or wish to make a data-related request, contact us at the email below and we will respond as required by applicable law.

7. Children's privacy

Trap Tap is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your region). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through the App, please contact us so we can address it.

8. Security

Data transmitted by the advertising service is encrypted in transit using industry-standard TLS. No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
